How This AI Works

This is a Multimodal Emotion Analysis System that understands you through:

  • Text – Sentiment analysis (positive/negative) + Intent detection
  • Image – Face emotion recognition (happy, sad, angry, fear, surprise, disgust, neutral)
  • Voice – Emotion from audio (happy, sad, angry, stressed, tired, neutral)
  • Webcam – Real‑time face emotion detection (same 7 emotions)

🧠 What is RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)?
When you ask a factual question (e.g., "what is stress?"), the AI first searches its internal knowledge base (knowledge_base.txt) for the most relevant information. It then uses that to generate a precise, helpful answer – instead of making things up. This makes the AI smarter and more reliable.

🚀 Try asking: "what is stress?", "how to calm anger?", or upload a sad/happy image. The system also detects tired, stressed, anxious, and more from voice!
